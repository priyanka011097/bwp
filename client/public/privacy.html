Privacy & Cookies Policy

Last updated: 2026

This Privacy & Cookies Policy explains how Build With Priyanka.in ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects the information you provide when you visit this website or get in touch with us.

1. Information we collect

We only collect information you choose to share, mainly through the contact form:

  • Your name
  • Your email address or phone number
  • Details about your project, idea, and budget

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

2. How we use your information

  • To respond to your enquiry and discuss your project.
  • To prepare proposals and deliver agreed work.
  • To keep in touch about your project where relevant.

3. Cookies

This website uses only the minimal cookies and local storage required for the site to function. We do not use cookies for advertising. If we add analytics in the future, this policy will be updated and, where required, your consent requested.

4. Third parties

We may use trusted service providers (for example, hosting and email delivery) purely to operate the website and respond to you. These providers process data only on our behalf.

5. Data retention

We keep enquiry information only as long as needed to respond and, where a project proceeds, for the duration of our working relationship and any legal record-keeping requirements.

6. Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal information we hold about you at any time by contacting us.

7. Contact

For any privacy questions, reach out via WhatsApp at +91 89835 69162 or through the contact form on the homepage.
